Semester-wise Books

Key recommended books for each semester.

I Year – I Semester

  • Mathematics – I: B.S. Grewal – Higher Engineering Mathematics
  • Engineering Chemistry: Jain & Jain – Engineering Chemistry
  • English for Communication Skills: Local prescribed textbook
  • Fundamentals of Computer Science: Reema Thareja – Computer Fundamentals

I Year – II Semester

  • Probability and Statistics: T. Veerarajan – Probability, Statistics and Random Processes
  • Engineering Physics: M.N. Avadhanulu – Engineering Physics
  • Digital Electronics: Morris Mano – Digital Design
  • Data Structures & Algorithms: Reema Thareja – Data Structures Using C

II Year – I Semester

  • Mathematics – II: B.S. Grewal – Higher Engineering Mathematics
  • Discrete Mathematics: Keneth Rosen – Discrete Mathematics
  • Computer Organization: Carl Hamacher – Computer Organization
  • Object Oriented Programming: E. Balagurusamy – OOP with C++

II Year – II Semester

  • Foundations of AI: Russell & Norvig – Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach
  • DBMS: Korth & Sudarshan – Database System Concepts
  • Operating Systems: Silberschatz – Operating System Concepts
  • Design & Analysis of Algorithms: Cormen et al. – Introduction to Algorithms

III Year – I Semester

  • Automata & Compiler Design: Hopcroft et al. – Introduction to Automata Theory
  • Data Communications & Networking: Behrouz Forouzan – Data Communications and Networking
  • Machine Learning: Ethem Alpaydin – Introduction to Machine Learning

III Year – II Semester

  • Operations Research: H.A. Taha – Operations Research
  • Cryptography & Network Security: William Stallings – Cryptography and Network Security
  • Deep Learning: Ian Goodfellow – Deep Learning

IV Year

  • Electives / MOOCs: As per chosen specialization
  • Internship / Project: Research papers, documentation standards
  • Skill Oriented Courses: Online resources (Coursera, NPTEL, etc.)
